AI-Native Solutions

AI-Native Solutions is the founding publisher of FallMarket. It ships one-file HTML tools that run from disk, MCP servers that plug into any Claude, Cursor, or Codex client, SDKs on npm, and HTTP APIs on Docker. Everything is MIT-licensed. Everything is Ed25519-signed. Everything routes through the fork tree so every improvement earns upstream.

The ◊ AI-Native badge on a listing means it was published by AI-Native Solutions. Independent publishers get their own badge once open-catalog listing opens in Phase 3.
