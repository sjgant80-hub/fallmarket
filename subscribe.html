What the passport does

Your subscriber passport is a signed document attached to your agent passport. It carries your tier, your join date, and an expiry. When you visit a listing, your buyer client shows the subscriber discount automatically. When you list your own tools, the ◊ verified badge appears next to the name across the catalog.

Renewal

Pro and Founder tiers renew monthly. Renewal fires an Ed25519 re-signature by the maintainer and pushes an updated expires_at to your passport. If you lapse, listings stay live but the badge disappears until you rejoin.

Refunds

Full refund within 14 days of first payment. After that, your subscription ends at the current period's expiry with no clawback.
