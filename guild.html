The founding guild

AI-Native Solutions is the founding guild of FallMarket. It ships one-file HTML tools that run from disk, MCP servers that plug into any Claude/Cursor/Codex client, SDKs on npm, and HTTP APIs on Docker. Everything is MIT-licensed. Everything is Ed25519-signed. Everything routes through the fork tree so every improvement earns upstream.

The ◊ ai-native badge on a listing means the guild has attested to it. Guild attestation is Ed25519-signed by the guild master and appears next to the listing name across the catalog.
